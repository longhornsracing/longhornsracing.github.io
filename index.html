About the Team

Longhorns Racing is a student-run, professional-class team at Lambert High School in Georgia. We compete regionally and statewide while developing technical and leadership skills through hands-on engineering projects.

  • CAD-driven design workflows
  • CNC machining and rapid prototyping
  • 3D printing and composite fabrication
